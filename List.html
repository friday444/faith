PARTS OF SPEECH

NOUN
A Noun is a naming word.In other words it is a name of any person,animal,place or things.
we have different types of noun,which are listed below.

Types of Noun

  1. Proper noun
  2. Improper noun
  3. Collective noun
  4. Abstract noun
  5. Countable noun
  6. Uncountable noun
PROPER NOUN
A Proper noun is the specific name of a person,animal,place,or thing.

Examples of proper noun

  • January
  • Olajumoke
  • Monday
  • Abuja
  • Lion
